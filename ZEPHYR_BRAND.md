# Zephyr Brand System

## Positioning

Zephyr Card is a Bahamas-first card-linked rewards platform for the Caribbean–US corridor.

Zephyr is not just a card. Zephyr is the customer, rewards, partner, and corridor intelligence layer. Issuing and banking partners provide regulated card infrastructure. Zephyr owns the customer experience, rewards logic, partner network, and data layer.

## Brand Feeling

- Premium Caribbean fintech
- Institutional but warm
- Trustworthy
- Elegant
- Motion-led but restrained
- Built around compass, corridor, card, and rewards symbolism

## Visual Motifs

- Compass pointer

## Motion Principles

- Subtle fade and slide
- Slow compass motion
- Soft hover lift
- Staggered card reveals
- No childish bounce
- No generic AI gradients
- No fake 3D people

## Legal / Brand Guardrails

Do not claim:

- Zephyr is live
- A production issuing partner is confirmed
- A bank partnership is confirmed
- Scene+ or Scotiabank is a partner
- Rewards are guaranteed

Use:

- Designed to
- Building
- Pilot environment
- Selected partners
- Eligible activity
- Participating partners
